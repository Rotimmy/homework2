About Me

Hi, I'm Boogie! I'm a chimpanzee. That makes us cousins, fam.

I'll tell you what: it sure is tough being a chimp in a man's world - I constantly feel pigeon-holed and underestimated. But you know what? There's a lot more to me than just eating bananas and throwing poo. In fact, I have much more in common with all of you people out there than so most of you realize. For instance, did you know chimps and humans share 98.8 percent of our genetic makeup? For real, cuz. I bet you didn't even realize you shared that much DNA with Donald Trump!
